Who We Are

Amrekan Innotech is a leading turnkey project consultancy firm, delivering world-class design, execution, and validation services across regulated sectors. With 25+ years of experience, our multidisciplinary team has executed complex Greenfield and Brownfield projects with precision.

Our Director has served industries such as pharmaceuticals, API, biotech, R&D labs, food, cosmetics and logistics. Our projects adhere to global standards like USFDA, WHO-GMP, MHRA, TGA, and Schedule M, ensuring compliance and operational efficiency from concept to commissioning.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted engineering and design partner for turnkey industrial infrastructure in regulated environments.

Our Mission

To deliver smart, cost-efficient, and regulation-compliant solutions for industrial facilities using innovation, integrity, and interdisciplinary expertise.
